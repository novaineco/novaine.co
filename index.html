OVERVIEW OF THE WEBSITE

’90s fashion trends were heavily influenced by the rise of the internet and Style Network, a cable channel broadcasting beauty-centric shows. Suddenly, fashion was more accessible to everyone, and luxury brands like Versace and Tommy Hilfiger were no longer reserved for celebrities and the elite.

Welcome to Novaine's Retro Vibe, your one-stop shop for everything 90s fashion. Browse our carefully chosen selections of retro-inspired apparel, accessories, and shoes that perfectly encapsulate 90s style. The greatest of 90s fashion is delivered directly to your closet with Novaine's designs, whether you're wanting to relive your favorite decade or find classic pieces.

The ’90s was a golden era of pop culture and music. Known as the decade of technological innovation, multiculturalism, and reality TV, the ’90s sparked a wave of minimalist looks, self-expression, and nostalgia. It’s also the decade when hip-hop culture was at its peak.

ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Discover the essence of 90s fashion with our collection of inspired clothing. This website explores a timeless style that will add touch of nostalgia to your look. This website was established in January 2025 that will help you bring back the nostalgic feels of fashion. We, the Novaine, are your guide to your Everyday fashion.

The Proponents

This website was created by 6 students enrolled in Bacherlor of Science in Office Administration program in academic year 2024-2025

Proponents chose the 1990s because fashion trends at the time were renowned for relaxed and casual styles, such as apparel with darker color palettes and simpler patterns influenced by international pop culture, such as music and television. Denim was the fabric of choice throughout this period. It represents both casualness and versatility in everyday wear.



Novaine Co. was developed by 2nd year BSOA2-1D students at Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
